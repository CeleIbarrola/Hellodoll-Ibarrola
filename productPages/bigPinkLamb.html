Big Pink Lamb

Cute plush stuffed lamb rag doll

50 USD

This plushie sheep cloth toy will be the perfect adventure (and mischief) companion for your kids. It is also ideal if you want a unique and original baby shower gift.

This softie lamb, was created in a colorful floral microfiber towel of great durability and resistance. The face is hand-embroidery with thread and it has a fluffy tail and a pink plush scarf and a little bow in the ear. It is stuffed with non-allergenic polyester fiber, in a in 100% smoke free studio. It is 29cm-11,41 inch tall without measuring. (Since it's handmade it can vary a little bit). I thoroughly design and make all of my items with great care, dedication and special attention to details. Usually Hellodoll designs change as they come to life, so this cuddly doll is one of a kind creature, he has a unique feel and look.
