Big Light Blue Lion

Stuffed cuddle lion rag doll

50 USD

Meet the newest addition to our jungle family! This cute stuffy lion King doll, is a soft one of a kind textile art doll, perfect for a safari animals theme nursery decor or as a baby shower newborn boy gift.

This fabric lion toy, was created with a stripped micro-corderoy cotton fabric of great durability and resistance. The face is hand-embroidery with thread and it has a soft fur head, a long tail and a light blue microfiber fabric scarf, with a wood button. It is stuffed with non-allergenic polyester fiber, in a in 100% smoke free studio. It is 28 cm-11,02 inch tall. (Since it's handmade it can vary a little bit) I thoroughly design and make all of my items with great care, dedication and special attention to details. Usually Hellodoll designs change as they come to life, so this cuddly doll is one of a kind creature, he has a unique feel and look.
